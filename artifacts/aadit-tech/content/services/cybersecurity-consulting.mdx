# Cybersecurity Consulting

Our cybersecurity consulting practice helps organisations in regulated industries build
robust security postures aligned with global frameworks such as ISO 27001, SOC 2, HIPAA,
and RBI guidelines.

## What We Offer

- **Risk Assessment & Gap Analysis** — Identify vulnerabilities and prioritise remediation.
- **Penetration Testing** — Web, mobile, API, and infrastructure pen testing.
- **Security Architecture Review** — Cloud and on-premise security design.
- **Compliance Readiness** — ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, PCI-DSS preparation.
- **Incident Response Planning** — Playbooks, tabletop exercises, and retainer support.

## Who We Serve

We specialise in serving healthcare providers, BFSI institutions, fintech startups, and
SaaS companies that handle sensitive data and must meet stringent regulatory requirements.
