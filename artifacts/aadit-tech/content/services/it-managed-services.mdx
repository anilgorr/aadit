# IT Managed Services

Aadit Technologies provides 24/7 managed IT services designed to keep your business
running reliably while reducing operational overhead.

## Service Scope

- **Infrastructure Monitoring & Management** — Real-time visibility across servers, networks, and cloud resources.
- **Endpoint Management** — Device lifecycle, patching, and security hardening.
- **Cloud Operations** — AWS, Azure, and GCP management and cost optimisation.
- **Help Desk Support** — Tiered support for end-users and technical teams.
- **Backup & Disaster Recovery** — Automated backups with tested recovery procedures.

## SLA Commitments

All managed services engagements include defined SLAs for uptime, response time,
and resolution time — tailored to your business criticality requirements.
